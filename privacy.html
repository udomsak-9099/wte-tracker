Privacy Policy

WTE Tracker — Construction Management System
Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

WTE Tracker is a construction management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

WTE Tracker does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.

Data Storage

  • All project data (tasks, permits, reports, settings) is stored locally on your device only.
  • No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • You can export and import your data using the built-in backup feature.
  • Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app will delete all stored data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services for functionality only:

  • Open-Meteo API — for weather data (no personal data is sent, only location coordinates for the project site).
  • Google Fonts — for the Inter font family.

User Accounts

User accounts created within the app are stored locally on the device. No registration with any external service is required.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
